Your Purchase

Lender Costs

Underwriting, processing, and any points. Typically 0.5–1% of the loan amount.

Professional & Title

NJ buyers typically pay $1,500–$2,500 through attorney review and closing.

One-time premium set by NJ rate schedules; roughly 0.4–0.6% of price for most purchases.

Add $100–$300 more for radon, oil tank sweep, or termite in older housing stock.

Prepaids & Escrow

Used to estimate the ~3 months of taxes typically collected at closing.
