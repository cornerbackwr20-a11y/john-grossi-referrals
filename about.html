Who I am

I'm John, a New Jersey licensed real estate referral agent. Rather than listing and showing homes myself, I focus on one thing: matching buyers and sellers with the right agent for their specific situation, based on real performance data rather than who has the biggest marketing budget.

What a referral agent is (and isn't)

Under New Jersey real estate law, a referral agent is licensed through a sponsoring broker and may only refer buyers and sellers to that broker for placement with an active agent — a referral agent is not permitted to show property or represent either side of a transaction. In practice, that means:

  • You never pay me anything — referral fees are paid by the receiving brokerage out of their commission after closing, standard industry practice.
  • I have no incentive to steer you toward a specific listing or a faster-but-worse deal, because I'm not the one doing the transaction.
  • Every match still goes through a full-service, actively licensed agent who handles your entire transaction from showing to closing.
Licensing disclosure: John Grossi Referrals is operated by John Grossi, a New Jersey licensed real estate referral agent affiliated with Shore Properties Referral LLC, NJ license #2185031. Referral agents are limited by N.J.S.A. 45:15-1 et seq. to referring prospective clients to their sponsoring broker and may not show, list, or negotiate the sale of real property.
