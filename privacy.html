Who we are

This website is operated by John Grossi, a New Jersey licensed real estate referral agent affiliated with Shore Properties Referral LLC (NJ license #2185031). You can reach us at John.T.Grossi@gmail.com or (848) 216-8484.

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide through the forms on this site, which may include your name, phone number, email address, whether you are buying or selling, target towns, and any property or transaction details you share. We also collect limited technical information automatically through analytics tools (see "Cookies and analytics" below), such as pages visited, device type, and general location.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your request and match you with a receiving real estate agent through our sponsoring broker.
  • To share relevant details with the receiving agent and brokerage so they can serve you — this is the core purpose of a referral.
  • To send you the resources you request (such as PDF guides) and occasional market updates by email.
  • To contact you by phone or email about your request, where you have agreed to this.
  • To measure and improve how this website and our advertising perform.

We do not sell your personal information.

Phone calls and email

If you check the consent box on our contact form, you agree to be contacted by phone and email by John Grossi Referrals about your request. You can ask us to stop contacting you at any time by emailing John.T.Grossi@gmail.com.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use Google Analytics, Google Ads conversion tracking, and the Meta (Facebook) Pixel. These tools use cookies or similar technologies to help us understand how visitors use the site and to measure advertising performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings, opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, and manage Meta ad preferences in your Facebook account settings.

How your information is handled

Form submissions are delivered to us via Web3Forms, a form-processing service, and arrive in our email inbox. Information you provide may be shared with our sponsoring broker and the receiving agent as part of completing a referral. We retain your information as long as needed to serve your request and to meet record-keeping obligations that apply to licensed referral activity in New Jersey.

Your choices

  • You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (subject to legal record-keeping requirements).
  • You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in the email or by contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these choices, email John.T.Grossi@gmail.com.

Children

This site is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with a revised date at the top of the page.
